My Story

It all started with my parents telling me that I could become whoever I wanted to become. As a forklift operator, I began to remember those words. Desiring to learn new things and challenge myself more, I decided to start learning to code January of 2016. On September 26th, 2016, I took a huge leap of faith and registered as a student at The Iron Yard Academy. On December 16th, 2016, I graduated The Iron Yard Academy with a Front-End Engineering Certification. Now on my way to becoming a web developer in greater Nashville area, I am beginning to grow into the person I desired to become. With my insatiable desire to learn and grow, the next chapter will be fueled with mentors challenging me in new technologies, growing my understanding in programming, and beginning to build the most advanced web applications in the industry. When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending time with my wife and son, and serving in the community. I actively volunteer with my church, Water's Edge, on Saturday nights as the worship director
